About This App

The "Class Marks Manager" app helps teachers and students efficiently manage class marks. Users can add, view, and remove marks across multiple subjects, as well as calculate the average marks per student. All data is securely stored in Local Storage for convenience and accessibility.

Key Features:

  • Add and manage marks for subjects like Math, History, English, Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Geography, Civic, Philosophy, Economics, Sociology, Religion, Activities, Arabic, and Library.
  • View individual subject grades and overall averages for each student.
  • Remove student marks easily with a click of a button.
  • Responsive design ensuring accessibility on various devices.
